About Me

Hey there! I'm Negin, but you can call me Neginz or just Ginz—I answer to both! My journey brought me to the world of front-end development, ignited by my attempt to recreate the IMDB website during my university days—a clear reflection of my passion for series. I'm captivated by visuals, colors, and graphic design, having delved into these realms with genuine excitement. Organizing things, especially when it involves sheets and data, is where I thrive. My master's thesis ventured into text mining using Rapidminer, and I further honed my skills in a remote job for a business consulting and services company in another country, maintaining their online data. Beyond coding, I venture into audio editing and social media management for friends' podcasts. Amidst my diverse interests, front-end development remains my professional passion, while design and editing serve as my creative escapes. So, if you're seeking a dynamic blend of coding enthusiasm and creative flair, look no further!
